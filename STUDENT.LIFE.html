Student Life at HLGU

Get involved, make lifelong friendships, and experience everything HLGU has to offer beyond the classroom!

Clubs & Organizations

Join a variety of student-led clubs and organizations to make the most of your college experience.

  • Student Government Association (SGA): Represent the student body and organize events.
  • Campus Ministries: Engage in spiritual growth, missions, and community service.
  • Honors Society: Connect with fellow high-achieving students.
  • Intramural Sports: Enjoy friendly competitions in basketball, soccer, and volleyball.
  • Fine Arts Club: Explore music, theater, and visual arts.
  • Technology & Coding Club: Dive into programming, robotics, and tech projects.

Housing & Dining

Campus Housing

HLGU offers comfortable residence halls with modern amenities. Each dorm is designed to provide a safe and supportive living environment.

  • Men's & Women's Dorms: Fully furnished rooms with Wi-Fi access.
  • Apartment-Style Housing: Available for upperclassmen.
  • Resident Assistants (RAs): Support and guidance available on every floor.

Dining Options

Enjoy a variety of delicious and healthy meal options at our dining facilities.

  • Main Dining Hall: Buffet-style meals with fresh, nutritious choices.
  • Café & Coffee Shop: Grab-and-go meals, snacks, and drinks.
  • Special Dietary Options: Vegetarian, gluten-free, and allergen-friendly meals available.
