Student Life

Experience the vibrant and supportive community that makes HLGU home. Get involved, connect, and thrive!

Discover Your Home Away From Home

At Hannibal-LaGrange University, student life is about more than just attending classes. It's about creating lasting memories, building lifelong friendships, and growing as an individual in a Christ-centered environment. From exciting campus events to a wide array of clubs and organizations, there's something for everyone to enjoy and get involved in.

Campus Activities & Events

Enjoy a wide range of events, from concerts and movie nights to special holiday celebrations. Get involved and make memories that will last a lifetime!

Clubs & Organizations

Join a club that fits your interests! HLGU offers a diverse range of student-led organizations, from academic groups to social and service-oriented clubs.

Housing & Residence Life

Make yourself at home in our comfortable and welcoming residence halls. Experience community living and build friendships that will last a lifetime.

Student Support Services

HLGU is committed to supporting your academic, personal, and spiritual growth. Take advantage of our resources, including counseling services, career services, and academic advising.
