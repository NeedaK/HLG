About Hannibal-LaGrange University

Our Mission

Hannibal-LaGrange University is a four-year Christian university accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. With Christ as its foundation, HLGU offers accessible, caring, and challenging experiences which prepare students for lives of service and leadership.
