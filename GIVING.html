Expand Your Network and Stay Connected with Fellow Trojans!

HLGU Alumni Connect is more than just a platform — it’s your gateway to a lifelong Trojan community. Reconnect with classmates, rekindle meaningful friendships, and stay in the loop with career milestones, life updates, and exclusive alumni-only resources. Whether you’re just starting your career, changing paths, or looking to mentor others, Alumni Connect helps you build valuable relationships that last a lifetime.

Did you know HLGU has alumni living and working in cities all around the world? No matter where life takes you, chances are a fellow Trojan isn’t far away. Find and connect with others in your area or professional field — you never know where the next opportunity or friendship might come from.

Have questions or want to get involved?

Reach out to us at Alumni@hlgu.edu — we’d love to hear from you!

Invest in the future of HLGU and its students! Your generosity can help provide scholarships, enhance academic programs, and improve campus facilities.

Every gift, big or small, makes a difference in the lives of our students.

Ways to Give:

  • Online: Make a secure gift online through our Online Giving Form.
  • By Mail: Send a check payable to Hannibal-LaGrange University to:
    Hannibal-LaGrange University
    2800 Palmyra Road
    Hannibal, MO 63401
  • By Phone: Call our Advancement Office at (573) 629-3126 to make a gift over the phone.
  • Planned Giving: Consider including HLGU in your estate plans. Learn more about planned giving options.
  • Gifts of Stock: Donate appreciated stock to HLGU for potential tax benefits. Contact the Advancement Office for transfer instructions.

Areas to Support:

  • The HLGU Fund: Provides unrestricted support for the University's greatest needs.
  • Scholarships: Help make an HLGU education accessible to deserving students.
  • Academic Programs: Support specific departments and programs, such as Nursing, Education, or Music.
  • Athletics: Contribute to the success of our Trojan athletic teams.
  • Capital Projects: Help us improve campus facilities and infrastructure.

Your gift, no matter the size, is an investment in the lives of our students and the future of Hannibal-LaGrange University. Donate Now!

Shine a Light on Your Success!

HLGU is proud of our alumni and their accomplishments! This is your opportunity to share your journey, inspire fellow Trojans, and connect with the HLGU community.

Share Your Story:

Have you achieved a professional milestone, made a difference in your community, or have an inspiring story to share? We want to hear from you!

To submit your story for consideration:

  • Write a brief summary of your accomplishment or story (200-300 words).
  • Include a high-resolution photo of yourself.
  • Email your submission to: Alumni@hlgu.edu with the subject line "Alumni Spotlight Submission."
