Why choose HLGU?

No matter where you are on your academic journey—whether you're a recent high school graduate, pursuing a graduate degree, returning to complete your education, transferring credits, or still in high school—HLGU welcomes you.

At HLGU, students grow both personally and professionally in a uniquely Christian environment, equipping them for success in life and career.

Come Visit Us on Trojan Days

Trojan Day is your opportunity to experience what HLGU has to offer firsthand. Designed with you and your family in mind, this event allows you to explore campus, connect with current students, engage with faculty, attend a class, and enjoy lunch in the cafeteria. Packed with valuable experiences, the day is crafted to help you envision yourself as a Trojan at Hannibal-LaGrange University!

Personal Campus Visit

We can't wait to meet you and show you around campus! Come connect with the people who make HLGU special and explore the opportunities that await you here. Spend a day with us, and you'll experience the distinctively Christian, high-quality education that makes HLGU stand out.
