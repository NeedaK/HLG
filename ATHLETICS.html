Athletics

Elevate your college experience! Compete, connect, and build character through HLGU Athletics.

Join the Trojan Family: A Winning Tradition

At Hannibal-LaGrange University, athletics is more than just games; it's about developing well-rounded individuals who excel both on and off the field. We are committed to providing a supportive and Christ-centered environment where athletes can reach their full potential. Discover a community that values teamwork, sportsmanship, and academic excellence.

Why Choose HLGU Athletics?

Competitive Teams

Participate in a wide range of sports at the NAIA level. Showcase your talent and compete against top athletes.

Outstanding Facilities

Train in state-of-the-art facilities designed to enhance your performance and support your athletic development.

Dedicated Coaches

Learn from experienced and passionate coaches who are committed to your athletic and personal growth.

Academic Support

Receive the resources and support you need to succeed in the classroom while balancing your athletic commitments.

Community & Camaraderie

Become part of a close-knit team and a supportive campus community. Forge lasting friendships and create unforgettable memories.

Character Development

Develop leadership skills, discipline, and a strong work ethic. Learn values that will serve you well throughout your life.
